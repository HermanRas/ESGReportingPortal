Scope Definitions
Scope 3 GHG emissions
are direct emissions from sources that are owned or controlled by the reporting company (GHG Protocol).
Scope 2 GHG emissions
are the emissions associated with the generation of electricity, heating/ cooling, or steam purchased for own consumption.
Scope 3 GHG emissions
are “other indirect emissions that are a consequence of the reporting company, but occur at sources owned or controlled by another company” (GHG Protocol).
